Research Interests

I am focusing on the large-scale machine learning systems, I am currently work on LLM systems, aiming to reach fault-tolerant, efficient, low-cost distributing training, inference, and serving systems for LLMs. My dream is to make AIGC affordable to be running on scalable devices and reach the diverse AI agents.

In my past two years of research experience, I have explored a wide range of directions, including:

  • ML Computing Paradigms: How to enable efficient management of emerging new workloads in heterogeneous datacenters and constructing systems for new computing paradigms.
  • SW-HW Co-design: The way how hardware, software, compiler, and data interact with each other to get optimized in specific ML tasks. ML tasks get either specifically or generally optimzied on emerging hardware.
  • Distributed systems for Large-scale ML: The infrastructure and systems we can build to enable fast and efficient training and inference, especially when LLM training/inference/serving is extremely costly today.
  • Deep Generative Models: how to use deep neural networks to model and generate data, such as images, sequences, and human motion, with the interaction with security and privacy.
